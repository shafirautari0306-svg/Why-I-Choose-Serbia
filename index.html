Scholarship Journey • Computer Science • International Dreams

Serbia represents more than just a destination for higher education to me. It is a place where I can grow academically, experience a new culture, and strengthen my knowledge in Computing & Software Development.

🎓

Quality Education

Serbia, especially through institutions such as University of Novi Sad, offers a dynamic environment for students pursuing Computing and Software Engineering, combining academic excellence, practical experience, and a growing international perspective.

🌍

International Environment

Interacting with students from different countries and backgrounds would expand my perspective, strengthen my adaptability, and help me understand how technology can connect people across cultures.

💻

Technology & Innovation

Personal Growth

Studying abroad would not only expand my academic opportunities, but also shape my personal growth by strengthening my adaptability, independence, and cross-cultural understanding.

My Personal Motivation

Growing up in Indonesia, I became aware that access to technology and digital information is still uneven across many communities. This realization inspired me to independently learn web development and explore how technology can be used to create practical and meaningful solutions.

Through studying in Serbia, I hope to expand my technical knowledge, experience an international learning environment, and continue developing solutions that promote greater digital inclusion.

My Future Goals

After completing my studies, I hope to continue developing digital solutions that improve access to information, technology, and digital opportunities for wider communities.

I believe studying in Serbia would become an important step in both my academic and personal growth, helping me build the knowledge and perspective needed to create meaningful impact in the future.
